VECNA

About Vecna.

The Whispered One, The Dying King, The Lord of the Rotted Tower, The Arch-Lich. He goes by many names and has many identities. Vecna is widely accepted to be the most powerful and the most evil lich in existence. He was once mortal but attained lichdom and eventually acheived godhood becoming the god of secrets.


Vecna is the king of all liches.

Lore of The Whispered One

  • Eons ago Vecna's lieutenant, Kas the Bloody-Handed, betrayed him. In their fight Kas removed Vecna's left-eye and left hand.
  • Vecna has had several apprentice but the most well known is Acererak the Eternal
  • His name is an anagram of the fantasy writer Jack Vance
